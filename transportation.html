Airport Transportation:

The hotel is close to the airport, approximately 2 miles away. Taxi service to the hotel will be under $10.00 one-way or the Cloud Nine Shuttle service for $6.50 one way.

If arriving at the airport, go to the transportation area at the airport and see the transportation coordinator with the red coat, and ask for the Cloud Nine Shuttle Service.

Driving Directions:

From Northeast:

Take I-15 South to 163 South. 163 will end and put you on 10th Avenue. Proceed to Broadway and turn right. Turn left on Broadway Circle. The hotel is on the right side.

From South:

Take I-5 North and exit at J Street which is a one-way street. Take J Street to Market Street and turn left. Proceed to first Avenue and turn right. Then turn right at E Street. The hotel is on the corner.

From East:

Take I-8 East to 163 South. 163 will end and put you on 10th Avenue. Proceed to Broadway and turn right. Turn left on Broadway Circle. The hotel is on the right side.

From Northwest:

Take I-5 and exit at Front Street which is a one-way street. Proceed to E Street and turn left. The hotel is on the right.
